About

Who Are We?

We are the Waterford Electronic Music Club, located at the Main Street across the Landmark. We have an extensive studio and multiple computers loaded up with music software along with MIDI keyboards. However, due to COVID-19 the studio is closed but we are offering classes over Zoom. We'll even help you set it up!

We are a group of passionate musicians who want to pass down the skills and knowledge for those who are interested in electronic muisc production. We enjoy teaching to those who are passionate and comitted. We offer 1-on-1 and group sessions but due to COVID-19 these will be done online.

We offer varying prices and tiers of classes. Please call us or contact us using the form here.
